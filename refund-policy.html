Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 2024

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are reviewed case by case for eligible issues such as damaged products, missing items, incorrect deliveries, or order problems reported within a reasonable time after delivery.

Cancellations

Cancellation requests should be made before dispatch. Once an order has been dispatched, cancellation may not be possible.

Damaged Or Incorrect Orders

Please contact us with order details and clear photos of the issue. After review, we may offer a replacement, refund, or another suitable resolution.

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are processed through the original or agreed payment method. Processing time may vary based on the payment provider or bank.

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds may not apply for issues caused by incorrect customer information, delayed reporting, opened or consumed products without a quality concern, or circumstances outside our control.
